1. Start “Recalibrate.exe”
2. Select serial setting and click ok
3. Click “Startadjust”
4. Check Led is flashing (please check led on transmitter)
5. Place trimmers:
	Left “Up-Down” – Up
	Left “Left-Rigth” – Rigth
	Rigth “Up-Down” – Up
	Rigth “Left-Rigth” – Rigth
6. Place stick of left end as follow  (Calibrate end points left and righ stick)
7. Place trimmers
	Left “Up-Down” – Down
	Left “Left-Rigth” – Left
	Rigth “Up-Down” – Down
	Rigth “Left-Rigth” – Left

8. Repeat step6
9. Click “Stopadjust”
10. Click “Open”
11. Select in directory program file “cool.tst”

12. Transmitter recalibrate!
